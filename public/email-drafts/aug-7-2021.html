Tonight is the night! I'm very excited to host our first hybrid online-and-outdoor-in-person dance party. My neighbors in Mosaic Commons have agreed to let us use the space outside their common house for our dance party at 8PM! To join and donate, please go to: https://www.shakeit.so

If you want to come in person, there are directions. We will also have a Zoom room, and part of the outdoor area will be marked off as the "Zoom Zone," so if you are in person and don't want to be on camera, you can still participate.

Tonight's event will be held to benefit Greater Boston Legal Services, with a suggested donation of any amount. GBLS does amazing work. Every day, GBLS attorneys and paralegals provide critical legal advice and representation to low-income individuals. If you look them up in the news right now, you'll see that they are representing many people who are facing eviction in the Boston area. This is right in our neighborhood, people are suffering, and they are helping. Please consider supporting GBLS.

Tonight's theme is Freedom and Respect. We'll have classics from Aretha Franklin to Queen, more recent hits from Beyoncé and John Batiste, and plenty more. I hope to see you there!

-Jonathan
